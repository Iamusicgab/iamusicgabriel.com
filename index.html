How I Started iamusic

I thought of the name "iamusic" as my love for this type of entertainment. And in the early years, iamusic was just a YouTube Channel full of blogs, gameplays, reviews, and more. But after many years, I decided that iamusic should be more than just being premiered on YouTube. Because of that, I pursued creating the second platform for iamusic, Plex. Plex is a global streaming media service made by Plex, Inc. With that, I had used their application and gradually increased the number of members. As I see the media in my server skyrocket, I rolled out "iamusic Originals" and "iamusic Top Priority" as a rating for the Movies and TV Shows within Plex. But this was no easy task. I had to download all of the movies that are requested manually. I wanted to download content that is requested automatically. As I searched through the internet, I found three applications, namely Ombi, Sonarr, and Radarr. These three combine to build a system of auto-download. As I think for another application to add to my ecosystem, I thought of creating a self-hosted E-Book Site. With that, I found an application called Ubooquity. Ubooquity is a server-hosted E-Book website. With this, I established the second application for iamusic. I also wanted to add a forums website where people can address their concerns, comment about something, and many more. That's where I chose phpBB to be my best application for my Forums Site. With these three applications, they make up the core system of iamusic.
